🔗 HOW TO CONNECT ICONS (Most Asked!)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

   1. HOLD SHIFT KEY (purple banner appears) 🟣
   2. CLICK first icon (turns green) 🟢
   3. KEEP HOLDING SHIFT
   4. CLICK second icon
   5. Connection appears! ➡️

   ⚠️  IMPORTANT: Don't release SHIFT between clicks!
